About us

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support

and increase the number of women in computer science. The organization

is working to close the gender gap in technology and change the image

of what a programmer looks like and does. They have a 7-week Summer

Immersion Program, a 2-week specialized Campus Program, after school Clubs,

and a 13-book New York Times best-selling series.The aim is to inspire,

educate, and equip young women with the computing skills to pursue 21st

century opportunities.

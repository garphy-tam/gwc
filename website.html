Basic stitches for beginners

Backstitch

Backstitch is the most basic kind of stitch that you can learn easily. It is best used to outline your images.

Running stitch

Running stitch creates dash lines and is often used for weaving and wrapping.

Chain stitch

Chain stitch forms a row of linked stitches that creates a bolder line of embroidery.

Satin Stitch

Satin stitch is a series of straight stitches that are next to each other to fill in an area.

Feather Stitch

Feather stitch is a linked stitch that creates open lines that make it look like they're moving. It's good for stitching seaweed, feathers, or scales.
